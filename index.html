About LWG Herbarium

About Digitization

The institute is converting physical specimens into comprehensive digital records utilizing high-resolution cameras and advanced scanners, ensuring long-term preservation and global research accessibility.

Specimen Collection Archive

The herbarium houses thousands of authenticated plant specimens collected from diverse ecological regions and preserved under scientific protocols for taxonomy and biodiversity studies.

AI Integration & Research

AI-powered identification systems enhance plant recognition accuracy, streamline taxonomic verification, and provide intelligent search capabilities for researchers worldwide.
